About Us

Welcome to our rowing data tracking app! We are passionate about rowing and fitness, and we created this app to help you track and analyze your rowing workouts.

Our goal is to provide you with the tools and insights you need to improve your rowing performance. Whether you're a competitive rower or rowing for fitness, our app is designed to help you achieve your goals.

If you have any questions or feedback, please don't hesitate to contact us. We'd love to hear from you and continue to improve our app.
